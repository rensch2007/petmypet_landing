Account Deletion Instructions

How to permanently delete your PetMyPet account and associated data.

We're sorry to see you go! If you no longer wish to use PetMyPet, you can easily delete your account directly from within the app. This process is fully automated and ensures that your data is removed from our active systems.

⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent.

Once you delete your account, it cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open the PetMyPet App

Launch the app on your iOS or Android device and ensure you are logged into the account you wish to delete.

2

Navigate to Settings

Tap on your profile icon in the bottom navigation bar, then tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner to open the Settings menu.

3

Select "Account"

Scroll down to the "Account" section and tap on it to view your account details.

4

Tap "Delete Account"

At the bottom of the Account screen, tap the red "Delete Account" button. You will be prompted to confirm your decision by entering your password.

What Data Gets Deleted?

When you confirm account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our active databases:

  • Profile Data: Your name, email, display name, and avatar.
  • Pet Profiles: All pets associated with your account, including their photos and details.
  • Walk History: All GPS routes, distances, durations, and activity markers.
  • Health Records: Health notes, weight logs, vaccination records, and calendar events.
  • Documents: Any uploaded medical records or certificates.
  • Social Data: Chat messages, playdate matches, and meetup history.
  • Achievements: All earned badges, streaks, and milestones.

Need Help?

If you no longer have access to the app, or if you encounter any issues while trying to delete your account, our support team can assist you.

Please send an email from the address associated with your PetMyPet account to:

contactpetmypet@gmail.com

We will process manual deletion requests within 30 days.
