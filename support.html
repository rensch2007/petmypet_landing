Frequently Asked Questions

After creating your account, tap the "+" icon on the Home dashboard or go to the Pets tab and select "Add Pet". You can enter your pet's name, species, breed, birthday, and upload a cute photo. You can add as many pets as you like!

Simply tap the "Start Walk" button on the Home screen. PetMyPet uses your phone's GPS to track your route, distance, and duration in real-time. You can also log water breaks and play stops along the way. When you're done, tap "Finish" to save the walk to your history.

Yes! Go to your pet's profile, tap "Settings", and select "Family Sharing". You can invite family members or caretakers via email. Once they accept, they'll be able to see the pet's profile, log walks, and add health notes.

PetMyPet allows you to track weight (with visual trend charts), log health notes with symptom tags, schedule vet visits and vaccinations, and store medical documents. You can even generate a one-tap report to share with your vet.

In the Discover tab, you can browse nearby pets. Our smart matching algorithm suggests playmates based on your pet's energy level, size, and temperament. If you find a match, you can chat with the owner to arrange a meetup.

You can delete your account at any time by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your data. For detailed instructions, visit our Account Deletion page.
