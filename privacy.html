Privacy Policy

At PetMyPet, we believe that trust is the foundation of any good relationship — whether it's with your pet or with the apps you use to care for them. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the PetMyPet mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible experience, we collect the following types of information:

Account Data

  • Credentials: Email address and password. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Profile Data (Optional)

  • User Details: Full name, birth year, sex/gender, display name, and avatar photo.
  • Location: Home location coordinates (used for playdate matching and local community features).

Pet Data

  • Basic Info: Name, species, breed, birth date, gender, weight, neutered status, and color/appearance.
  • Media & Health: Pet photos, medical notes, allergies, and preferred veterinary clinic information.

Walk & Location Data

  • Tracking: GPS route points (latitude, longitude, altitude, accuracy), walk duration, distance, and start/end locations.
  • Activity: Logs for water breaks, play stops, and weather conditions recorded during walks.

Health Data

  • Records: Health notes with symptom tags, weight logs, vaccination records, and medication schedules.
  • Calendar: Events for vet visits, grooming, and medications.

Documents

  • Files: Uploaded files (PDFs, images, Word/Excel documents) for medical records and vaccination certificates, which are securely stored in cloud storage.

Playdate & Social Data

  • Matching: Pet profile attributes (energy level, friendliness, play style, preferred sizes).
  • Interactions: Chat messages, meetup proposals, and match history with other users.

Device & Technical Data

  • Device Info: Push notification tokens and platform type (iOS or Android).
  • Health Platform Exports: Walking workouts written to Apple HealthKit or Android Health Connect (only when explicitly enabled by the user).
  • Locally Cached Data: Session tokens, app preferences, and cached API responses stored securely on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services to power core features of the PetMyPet app. These services only receive the data necessary to perform their specific functions:

  • Supabase: Used for our database, secure authentication, and file storage.
  • OpenWeatherMap: Provides weather and air quality data. It receives walk GPS coordinates to fetch local conditions.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Powers our push notifications and receives device tokens.
  • Apple HealthKit / Android Health Connect: Used for health data synchronization, strictly on an opt-in basis.
  • MapLibre: Renders maps for walk tracking. GPS coordinates are processed client-side only.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to operate, maintain, and improve the PetMyPet app. Specifically, your data is used for:

  • Providing accurate GPS walk tracking and route history.
  • Managing your pet's health records, reminders, and vet prep reports.
  • Facilitating social matching and playdate discovery with nearby pets.
  • Sending important push notifications regarding health reminders or chat messages.
  • Checking local weather and air quality conditions before you start a walk.
  • Integrating with native health platforms (Apple Health / Google Health Connect) if you choose to enable it.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With the third-party services listed above, strictly for app functionality.
  • Family Sharing: Pet co-owners and caretakers that you explicitly invite will have access to the shared pet's data, including walks, health records, and calendar events.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention: We retain your account data and associated content for as long as your account is active.

Deletion: You have the right to delete your account at any time directly from the app settings. Account deletion is permanent and immediately removes all profile data, pets, walks, documents, chat messages, matches, calendar events, health notes, weight logs, achievements, uploaded photos, and push tokens from our active databases.

6. Children's Privacy

The PetMyPet app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

7. Location Data

Precise GPS location data is collected only during active walks. This includes background tracking if you minimize the app while a walk is in progress. Location data is also optionally used to calculate distance for playdate matching. You maintain full control and can stop walk tracking or disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.

8. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. User passwords are securely hashed by Supabase Auth, and all uploaded files and documents are stored in secure cloud storage environments.

9. GDPR & CCPA Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU residents, or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents. These rights include:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data (achievable via the in-app deletion feature).
  • Right to Portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal data (Note: PetMyPet does not sell personal data).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our privacy team at:

Email: privacy@petmypet.app
