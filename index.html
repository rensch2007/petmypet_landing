Features

Everything your pet's life needs

From the morning walk to the vet visit — PetMyPet keeps you organized, connected, and motivated every step of the way.

🗺️

Walk Tracking

Never lose a step. PetMyPet records every walk with live GPS routing, real-time stats, and smart activity markers so you can relive every adventure.

  • Live GPS route tracking with real-time distance and duration
  • Activity markers for water breaks and play stops
  • Walk history with full route replay
  • Weather and air quality checks before heading out
  • Syncs with Apple Health and Google Health Connect

Health & Care

Keep your pet's entire health history in one place. From symptom notes to vaccination schedules, PetMyPet helps you stay one step ahead of your pet's wellbeing.

  • Health notes with symptom tags and severity tracking
  • Weight tracking with visual trend charts
  • Calendar for vet visits, vaccinations, medications, and grooming
  • Document storage for medical records and certificates
  • One-tap vet prep reports to share with your veterinarian

Your pet deserves a best friend too. Find compatible playmates based on energy level, size, and temperament — then chat and arrange meetups with nearby pet owners.

  • Browse nearby pet profiles and community posts

Family Sharing

Pet care is a team effort. Invite family members and caregivers to share pet profiles so everyone stays on the same page — no more missed medications or forgotten walks.

  • Invite unlimited family members and caregivers
  • Shared view of walks, health events, and care schedules
  • Real-time updates when any family member logs an event
  • Role-based access — owners and caretakers

How It Works

Get started in 3 simple steps

PetMyPet is designed to be as easy as a belly rub. Here's how to get going in minutes.

1
🐾

Create your pet's profile

Add your pet's name, species, breed, birthday, and a photo. PetMyPet supports a wide range of companions:

🐶 Dogs 🐱 Cats 🐦 Birds 🐰 Rabbits 🐹 Hamsters 🐠 Fish 🦎 Reptiles + more
2
📍

Start tracking walks & health

Tap to start a walk and watch your route come to life on the map. Log health notes, weight, medications, and vet visits — all in one place.

3
🤝

Discover playmates & connect

Browse compatible pets nearby, match, and chat with their owners. Propose meetups and build your local pet community.
